Quality

IPC-A-610D certification is used as the minimum standard for all of our manufacturing activities. In-House IPC instructor thereby ensuring the quality requirements dictated by IPC-A-610 are implemented throughout all applicable areas of the manufacturing process.

Ensuring our team fully understand the needs and expectations of our customers, thus ensuring their input is crucial in meeting and where possible exceeding these requirements. Utilising to the full our Software and Systems, by ensuring that effective process controls are implemented where necessary, and performance data is used to ensure continued quality performance, and ongoing improvement.
